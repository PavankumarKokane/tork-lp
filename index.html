Got questions?
We’ve got you covered!

How do I book the Kratos?

To book the Kratos, you must first register your details on our website. After creating an account, you must pay a refundable booking amount (₹999) to secure your spot. Our team will contact you to answer any queries and inform you about further processes. This amount will be adjusted in the total on-road price of the bike. Your payment is transferred to Tork Motors through a fully-secured payment gateway.

Will I be charged any fee for booking cancellation?

No, there is no fee for cancellation. Request a refund by writing to us at info@torkmotors.com with your booking ID. Our team will initiate the refund process, and the entire booking amount will be refunded to your account within 15 days.

What documents are required for the purchase of the bike?

You need to upload 3 documents for verification post-booking on our website. Keep your PAN Card, Aadhar Card, and Electricity bill. Once we quickly verify your documents, our team will inform you about the next steps to get your hands on the Kratos.

Can I still avail of the FAME-II subsidy to purchase the Kratos?

FAME-II subsidy benefit, as approved by the department of heavy industries, Govt. of India, is available on purchasing an electric vehicle. When asked, just share the OTP you will receive from the RTO & MHI with a Tork Motors executive. We will take care of the subsidy.

Do I have to pay extra for the Kratos charger and charger installation?

No, you will not be charged extra for the charger and charger installation. Once you complete the purchase, we will contact you before the Kratos is delivered. Our team will install the Kratos charger in your designated parking spot.
